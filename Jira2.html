If no knock-out occurs:

Knock-In Risk

Knock-In Price Level: N/A

Knock-In Type: N/A

Note: No knock-in risk exists, ensuring principal is returned in cash.

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If a Knock-In event occurs and (for AKI) the worst-performing stock is below the strike price at maturity, or (for EKI) the worst-performing stock is below the knock-in level at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Knock-In Risk: With AKI, a knock-in can occur anytime; with EKI, only at maturity if the worst-performing stock is below the knock-in level. If "N/A," no knock-in risk.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: CITI’s creditworthiness impacts repayment.
