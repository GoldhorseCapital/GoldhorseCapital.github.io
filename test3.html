Fixed Coupon Note (FCN) Product Specification

Issuer Details

Issuer Barclays
ISIN XS3078776609
Currency USD (United States Dollar)
Total Issue Size USD 460,000
Minimum Investment (Denomination) USD 46,000

Key Terms

  • Tenor: 6 months
  • Trade Date: July 31, 2025
  • Issue Date: August 7, 2025
  • Final Valuation Date: February 9, 2026

Underlying Stocks

The note’s performance is linked to the worst-performing of:

  • SNAP UN Equity: Initial price = USD 9.4800
  • MRVL UW Equity: Initial price = USD 82.7100

Coupon Payments

  • Annual Interest Rate: 12.07%
  • Periodic Coupon Rate: 1.0059% (paid in USD)
  • Coupon Payment Dates:
    • September 10, 2025
    • October 9, 2025
    • November 12, 2025
    • December 10, 2025
    • January 9, 2026
    • February 11, 2026

Example: For USD 46,000 invested, each coupon = USD 462.71

Knock-Out Feature (Early Termination)

  • Knock-Out Price Level: 88% of initial prices
  • Observation: Period End
  • Memory Effect: Enabled (True)

Outcome: If triggered, receive principal (USD 46,000) + accrued coupons.

Maturity Payout Conditions

If no knock-out occurs, on February 9, 2026, the payout depends on whether a Knock-In event occurred:

  • No Knock-In Event: Receive full principal (USD 46,000) + final coupon, regardless of the worst-performing stock’s price.
  • Knock-In Event Occurred:
    • Knock-In Price Level: 55.00%
    • Knock-In Type: European (EKI)

    If the worst-performing stock is below 55% on the final valuation date, which also means it is below 80% (Strike Price Level), you receive shares of the worst-performing stock (number of shares = USD 46,000 ÷ final stock price).

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If a Knock-In event occurs and the worst-performing stock is below the strike price at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Knock-In Risk: With EKI, a knock-in occurs only if the worst-performing stock is below the knock-in level on the final valuation date.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: Barclays’ creditworthiness impacts repayment.

Additional Notes

  • Valuation Dates: Monthly on the 7th or 8th
  • Knock-In: As the knock-in type is European, a knock-in occurs only if the worst-performing stock is below the knock-in level on the final valuation date, implying it’s also below the strike price, leading to share delivery.

This FCN offers regular income with principal protection unless a Knock-In event occurs and the worst-performing stock is below the strike price at maturity. Early termination is possible if all underlyings recover to initial levels.
