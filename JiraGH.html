Fixed Coupon Note (FCN) Product Specification

Issuer Information

Issuer CITI
ISIN XS3106604856
Currency USD (United States Dollar)
Total Issue Size USD 250,000
Minimum Investment (Denomination) USD 250,000

Key Terms

Tenor 3 months
Trade Date July 8, 2025
Issue Date July 15, 2025
Final Valuation Date October 15, 2025

Underlying Stocks

The note’s performance is linked to the worst-performing of:

  • TSLA UW Equity: Initial price = USD 297.00

Coupon Payments

Annual Interest Rate 16.11%
Periodic Coupon Rate 1.3425% (paid in USD)
Coupon Payment Dates
  • August 19, 2025
  • September 17, 2025
  • October 17, 2025

Example: For USD 250,000 invested, each coupon = USD 3,356.25

Knock-Out Feature (Early Termination)

Knock-Out Price Level 100% of initial prices
Observation Period End
Memory Effect Disabled
Outcome If triggered, receive principal (USD 250,000) + accrued coupons.

Maturity Payout Conditions

If no knock-out occurs:

  • On October 15, 2025, the payout depends on whether a Knock-In event occurred:
  • No Knock-In Event: Receive full principal (USD 250,000) + final coupon, regardless of the worst-performing stock’s price.
  • Knock-In Event Occurred: Not applicable as Knock-In feature is not present.

Knock-In Risk

No knock-in risk exists, ensuring principal is returned in cash.

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If the worst-performing stock is below the strike price at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Knock-In Risk: Not applicable.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: CITI’s creditworthiness impacts repayment.
