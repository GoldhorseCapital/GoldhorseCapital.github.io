Knock-Out Feature (Early Termination)

Knock-Out Price Level 88.00% of initial prices
Observation Daily
Memory Effect Enabled (True)
Outcome If triggered, receive principal (USD 46,000) + accrued coupons.

Maturity Payout Conditions

If no knock-out occurs:

  • On February 9, 2026, the worst-performing stock’s price is compared to:
  • Strike Price Level: 80.00%
    • Scenario 1: Worst stock ≥ 80.00% → Receive full principal + final coupon.
    • Scenario 2: Worst stock < 80.00% → Receive shares of the worst-performing stock (number of shares = USD 46,000 ÷ final stock price in USD).

Knock-In Risk

Knock-In Price Level 55.00%
Knock-In Type European

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If the worst-performing stock falls below 80.00% at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: Barclays’ creditworthiness impacts repayment.
