Fixed Coupon Note (FCN) Product Specification

Issuer Details

Issuer: Barclays

ISIN: XS3078776609

Currency: USD (United States Dollar)

Total Issue Size: USD 460,000

Minimum Investment (Denomination): USD 46,000

Key Terms

Term Details
Tenor 6 months
Trade Date July 31, 2025
Issue Date August 7, 2025
Final Valuation Date February 9, 2026

Underlying Stocks

The note’s performance is linked to the worst-performing of the following stocks:

  • SNAP UN Equity: Initial price = USD 9.4800
  • MRVL UW Equity: Initial price = USD 82.7100

Coupon Payments

Annual Interest Rate: 12.07%

Periodic Coupon Rate: 1.0059% (paid in USD)

Coupon Payment Dates:

  • September 10, 2025
  • October 9, 2025
  • November 12, 2025
  • December 10, 2025
  • January 9, 2026
  • February 11, 2026

Example: For USD 46,000 invested, each coupon = USD 462.71

Knock-Out Feature (Early Termination)

Knock-Out Price Level: 88% of initial prices

Observation: Period End

Memory Effect: Enabled

Outcome: If triggered, receive principal (USD 46,000) + accrued coupons.

Maturity Payout Conditions

If no knock-out occurs, on February 9, 2026, the payout depends on whether a Knock-In event occurred:

  • No Knock-In Event: Receive full principal (USD 46,000) + final coupon, regardless of the worst-performing stock’s price.
  • Knock-In Event Occurred (European: a stock is below 55% on the final valuation date):
    • Scenario 1: Worst-performing stock ≥ 80% → Receive full principal + final coupon.
    • Scenario 2: Worst-performing stock < 80% → Receive shares of the worst-performing stock (number of shares = USD 46,000 ÷ final stock price in USD).

Knock-In Risk

Knock-In Price Level: 55.00%

Knock-In Type: European

Note: For European, a knock-in event must occur if the worst-performing stock is below the knock-in price level on the final valuation date.

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If a Knock-In event occurs and the worst-performing stock is below 80% at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Knock-In Risk: With European, a knock-in can occur only at maturity. If not triggered, principal is returned in cash.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: Barclays' creditworthiness impacts repayment.
