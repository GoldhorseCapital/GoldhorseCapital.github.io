Fixed Coupon Note (FCN) Product Specification

Issuer Details

Issuer: Barclays

ISIN: XS3084885519

Currency: SGD (Singapore Dollar)

Total Issue Size: SGD 200,000

Minimum Investment (Denomination): SGD 50,000

Key Terms

  • Tenor: 6 months
  • Trade Date: August 5, 2025
  • Issue Date: August 19, 2025
  • Final Valuation Date: February 19, 2026

Underlying Stocks

The note’s performance is linked to the worst-performing of:

  • NVDA UW Equity: Initial price = USD 178.26
  • AVGO UW Equity: Initial price = USD 292.93

Coupon Payments

  • Annual Interest Rate: 8.00%
  • Periodic Coupon Rate: 0.6667% (paid in SGD)
  • Coupon Payment Dates:
    • September 23, 2025
    • October 22, 2025
    • November 21, 2025
    • December 23, 2025
    • January 22, 2026
    • February 23, 2026

Example: For SGD 50,000 invested, each coupon = SGD 333.35

Knock-Out Feature (Early Termination)

  • Knock-Out Price Level: 100% of initial prices
  • Observation: Daily
  • Memory Effect: Enabled
  • Outcome: If triggered, receive principal (SGD 50,000) + accrued coupons.

Maturity Payout Conditions

If no knock-out occurs:

  • On February 19, 2026, the worst-performing stock’s price is compared to:
  • Strike Price Level: 68.66%
  • Scenario 1: Worst stock ≥ 68.66% → Receive full principal + final coupon.
  • Scenario 2: Worst stock < 68.66% → Receive shares of the worst-performing stock (number of shares = SGD 50,000 ÷ final stock price in USD, converted to SGD).

Knock-In Risk

  • Knock-In Price Level: N/A
  • Knock-In Type: N/A

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If the worst-performing stock falls below 68.66% at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: Barclays' creditworthiness impacts repayment.
