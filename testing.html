Fixed Coupon Note (FCN) Product Specification

Issuer: Barclays

ISIN XS3092372856
Currency USD (United States Dollar)
Total Issue Size USD 300,000
Minimum Investment (Denomination) USD 300,000

Key Terms

Tenor 6 months
Trade Date August 15, 2025
Issue Date August 29, 2025
Final Valuation Date March 2, 2026

Underlying Stocks

The note’s performance is linked to the worst-performing of:

  • 9992 HK Equity: Initial price = HKD 272.90

Coupon Payments

Annual Interest Rate 17.56%
Periodic Coupon Rate 1.4634% (paid in USD)
Coupon Payment Dates
  • October 1, 2025
  • November 3, 2025
  • December 3, 2025
  • December 31, 2025
  • February 2, 2026
  • March 4, 2026

Example: For USD 300,000 invested, each coupon = USD 4,390.20

Knock-Out Feature (Early Termination)

  • Knock-Out Price Level: 100% of initial prices
  • Observation: Period End
  • Memory Effect: Disabled

Outcome: If triggered, receive principal (USD 300,000) + accrued coupons.

Maturity Payout Conditions

If no knock-out occurs:

  • On March 2, 2026, the worst-performing stock’s price is compared to:
    • Strike Price Level: 70.00%
    • Scenario 1: Worst stock ≥ 70.00% → Receive full principal + final coupon.
    • Scenario 2: Worst stock < 70.00% → Receive shares of the worst-performing stock (number of shares = USD 300,000 ÷ final stock price in HKD, converted to USD).

Knock-In Risk

  • Knock-In Price Level: 70.00%
  • Knock-In Type: European

Key Risks

  • Market Risk: If the worst-performing stock falls below 70.00% at maturity, you may receive shares worth less than your principal.
  • Early Termination Risk: Knock-out may limit total interest earned.
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations may affect share value if delivered.
  • Issuer Risk: Barclays’s creditworthiness impacts repayment.
